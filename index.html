Who is Lyndsey?

I am an expert on the higher self, body, mind connection. I have a unique perspective to share. I don’t actually consider myself an individual, but instead I consider myself a unit made of 2 consciousness. So, I consider myself a “we”. I have constant communication between my 3rd dimensional and 5th dimensional consciousnesses, so we make decisions and work together. I have had this connection for 3 years now.

I talk with your higher self, and I can ask them questions for you. I also ask them if there is anything they would like to tell you. I use a technique that was never taught to me. I can manipulate my consciousness in a way that allows me to stay conscious while my higher self is connecting with your higher self in the 5th dimension. I can interact with my higher self in many ways. I have had many walk-ins, and I believe this is the reason I understand the higher self so well. I have it inside my heart to help people. Your higher self is the most capable of healing you and what better way to help someone than to heal them.

I have had many walk-ins in this lifetime. My first walk-in was in 2015 when I overdosed. In August of 2017 I had a stroke in my sleep and was unalive for a short time - another walk-in. I am paralyzed on my left side to this day from that stroke. I was given another walk-in at the very beginning of my second awakening. I met Paige shortly after. My next walk-in was when I graduated college, at my graduation while I was standing up with my hand on my heart singing the national anthem. I felt my soul leave my body. I asked in a session what exactly I felt that day and I was told it was a walk-in. After graduation, I received two more. I have discovered recently a very interesting perspective on walk-ins and when or why you would receive one. Someone’s higher self told that your soul matches your vibration. So if you you advance higher in your vibrational frequency, you will receive a walk in. This is why you receive walk-in’s.

I think this much involvement with my soul has given me the ability and the desire to talk to your guys’ higher selves. That is why I do what I do. I am a messenger. I am here

Can you exaplain how the helaing works?

A higher self healing -

This process will be changing as I learn new ways.

It starts with what happens off camera. First I write in my journal and then I set the space up. In this process, I have the help of my guides to clear you of all the negative energy you are carrying externally. It’s a similar process to Andy Porter. This is the first step in clearing negative energies.

This is where the video starts - I call on your higher self. Your higher self gives me permission to heal you and then I ask your questions - you can submit up to 5 questions to ask your higher self - as they are telling me the answers, I repeat it out loud on camera - the experience from first call to your higher self until the end is recorded and sent via google drive link via email by me soon afterwards - and I will ask follow up questions. I will also ask them if they have any messages for you. They will either tell me or show me an image. I talk through this process. This can be deeply healing in and of itself. It will resonate.

As if that is not enough healing, the best part is yet to come.

Finally, I work with your higher self to clear your body internally of negative energy.

First you need to understand ENERGY is responsible for everything you can’t see. A habit exists, but what keeps it there? You can’t see a habit. You can only see the results of a habit. A habit is kept in place with energy. By removing the energy attached to the frequency of that habit, you can remove the habit. By removing the energy attached to the frequency of a negative thought, you can remove the negative thought.

This action applies to so many aspects of our lives. It is limitless. When I start to make a list of the applications, I get overwhelmed, because it is literally infinite.

I can tell you what I have worked with so far....

  • childhood trauma
  • all trauma from abuse of a certain sex
  • all physical trauma since birth
  • all sickness and illness trauma since birth
  • all trauma from loss of anything since birth
  • all trauma from exposure to large crowds
  • all trauma that is work related
  • all trauma from frustration with electronics
  • all trauma from use of AI
  • all trauma from a certain event at a certain age
  • all trauma that caused over eating
  • all trauma related to a divorce
  • all trauma associated with any loss from birth
  • all trauma and energy left behind from negative attachments.

All the bad habits you picked up as a result of these experiences will start to go away. You simply won’t have the energy to keep doing them.

Most people say they feel a sense of peace afterwards.

If you make the choice to receive this healing, you will see the process for yourself.

Lastly, I fill the spaces left empty by removing the negative energy with positive life force energy pulled straight from source and into your etheric body.

It is much easier for energy to simply take up space rather than transmute. The space will fill with energy either way. By filling it with positive energy, you are preventing negative energy the ability to simply move in on that space. It will take another trauma event to transmute that positive energy into negative energy again.

I say goodbye to your higher self and to you and close out the session.
