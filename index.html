My Healing Journey

I remember watching Tyler Henry the medium for the Stars when I was in my active addiction. I would get goosebumps watching him and at that time I didn’t feel anything. I even cried in happiness. Watching him was the only time I felt anything. Something inside of me activated. I didn’t know it at the time, but my soul was trying to reach me. That was the catalyst of my healing journey.

In August 2019, my first spiritual awakening occurred, but it took a wrong turn. Unbeknownst to me, a negative entity had been with me since childhood. During this time, I believed I was communicating telepathically with Tom Cruise, leading me to wait at gas stations, expecting him to meet me. In reality, I was interacting with this dark entity. This delusion persisted for a few months until I consciously chose to end it, realizing the energy was harmful. By simply ceasing to believe in it, I cut off this connection.

During this period, I found myself wielding what felt like superhuman abilities, which came naturally through engaging with this dark force. However, I was employing these powers in ways that violated universal laws. Eventually, I abandoned these capabilities, understanding their origin was malevolent. My higher self later revealed that relinquishing these powers was a critical step towards my next spiritual phase. Having lived most of my life in shadow, I recognized the darkness well; this time, I chose to ascend beyond it, leaving it behind for good.
